About

Dual‑licensed.
Deeply
committed.

Khushman is a dual-licensed lawyer qualified to practice in Ontario, Canada and before the Punjab and Haryana High Court in India. She provides practical, client-focused legal services and is committed to delivering clear and effective legal solutions tailored to each client’s needs.

As a sole practitioner, Khushman works closely with her clients to understand their goals and guide them through the legal process with professionalism, diligence, and attention to detail. She is dedicated to providing responsive service and strong advocacy while ensuring that clients feel informed and supported throughout their legal matter.

Khushman takes an empathetic and thoughtful approach with family law matters, recognizing that these issues involve sensitive and deeply personal circumstances. Her legal training across two jurisdictions provides valuable perspective when assisting individuals, families, and businesses with matters that extend beyond a single jurisdiction.

Solo practice means you always deal with Khushman directly. No handoffs. No associates. Your case stays with the person who took it.
