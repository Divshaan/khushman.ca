Who I Am

A lawyer who listens — and delivers.

I provide practical legal advice tailored to your specific situation. Whether you are navigating a real estate transaction, a family matter, an immigration process, or a business concern, I work to ensure you understand your options and feel supported throughout.

My practice is built on clear communication, efficient solutions, and responsive service. As a sole practitioner, I handle every file personally — from the first conversation to the final resolution. When you call, you reach me. When you need an update, you hear from me directly.
