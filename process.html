The Solo Difference

Why solo
works for you.

At a large firm, your file moves through departments. At Khushman Dhillon Law, it stays with one person — the person you hired.

Designed for direct communication
The solo practice model is built around direct access. The goal is that when you call, you reach your lawyer — not a switchboard or an assistant.
